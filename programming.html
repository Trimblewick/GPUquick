Just throw it into the oven

Ingredients:
Chicken breasts
Chorizo sausage
Zucchini
Pepper
Onions
Garlic
Chickpeas
Mixed olives

Carbs:
Couscous or rice

Spices:
Thyme
Black pepper
Salt
Oil
Sambal olek

Set oven to 175 degrees celsius.

Chop all choppable ingredients.
Pour all ingredients into a deep baking dish and just throw it into the oven.

It takes about 20 minutes to cook. Overcooked chicken will be dry (but do you really give a fuck?), undercooked chicken will kill you.

Serve with what ever carbs.

Beans with beans and chicken

Ingredients:
Chicken breasts
Onions
Chickpeas
Mixed beans
Coconut milk
Chopped Tomatos
Brocolli heads (frozen goes well)

Spices:
Thyme
Black pepper
Salt
Sambal olek
Garlic

Chop and fry onions and chicken breasts in a large pot.
Add all but the brocolli. As soon as the sauce is boiling add the brocolli.
