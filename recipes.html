Just throw it into the oven

Shopping list

Ingredients:
Chicken breasts - 500-1000 grams
Chorizo sausage - 300-500 grams
Zucchini
Pepper
Onions - 4-5
Garlic
Chickpeas - 1-2 cans
Mixed olives - 200 grams

Carbs:
Couscous or rice

Spices:
Thyme
Black pepper
Salt
Oil
Sambal olek

Instructions

Set oven to 175 degrees celsius.

Chop all choppable ingredients.
Pour all ingredients into a deep baking dish and just throw it into the oven.

It takes about 20 minutes to cook. Overcooked chicken will be dry (but do you really give a fuck?), undercooked chicken will kill you.

Serve with what ever carbs.

Thyme
Black pepper
Salt

Instructions

Find a large pot.

Chop and fry onions and chicken breasts in the pot.
Throw in all but the brocolli. Add the brocolli as soon as the sauce is boiling.
