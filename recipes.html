Shopping list

5 servings

Main ingredients:
Chicken breasts - 500-1000 grams
Chorizo sausage - 300-500 grams
Zucchini
Pepper
Onions - 4-5
Garlic
Chickpeas - 1-2 cans
Mixed olives - 200 grams

Carbs:
Couscous or rice

Spices:
Thyme
Black pepper
Salt
Oil
Sambal olek

Instructions

Set oven to 175 degrees celsius.

Chop all choppable main ingredients.
Pour the main ingredients into a deep baking dish and throw it into the oven.

It takes about 20 minutes to cook.

Serve with carbs.
